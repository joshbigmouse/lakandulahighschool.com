Lakan Dula High School (LDHS)

  • Lakan Dula High School (LDHS) is a public educational institution located in Tondo, Manila. It became independent from Florentino Torres High School as one of the annexes in 1962 and named after Lakan Dula, Manilaâ€™s last chieftain (lakan) before it was totally subjugated by the Spanish colonizers. The school provides quality, fair, culture-based and complete basic education to produce graduates who are intellectually and technologically competent. The school implements a K to 12 basic education curriculum that offers junior and senior high school. It is under the resolution ordered by the Department of Education (DepEd). It has Technical Vocational Livelihood (TVL) track specializes in the Home Economic strand and Industrial Arts strand which equips the students with technical skills for its senior high school program.
